When To Go

Kashmir Through the Seasons

Each season changes what the valley has to offer — plan around what you want to see.

Mar – May

Spring Bloom

Tulip gardens open in Srinagar, orchards blossom across the valley, and Doodhpathri's meadows turn green.

Jun – Aug

Summer Greens

Peak season for Gulmarg's meadows, Pahalgam's riverside walks, and family sightseeing across all five stops.

Sep – Nov

Autumn Gold

Chinar trees turn amber across Srinagar's gardens — fewer crowds, crisp air, dramatic valley light.

Dec – Feb

Winter Snow

Gulmarg becomes a skiing destination with snow-laden slopes; Srinagar's lakes take on a quieter, frozen calm.
